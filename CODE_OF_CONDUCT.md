# 📜 Code of Conduct

## 🌟 Our Goal

We want to keep this project respectful, creative, and easy to collaborate on.

---

## 👍 Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Give clear and helpful feedback
- Stay on topic
- Respect creative work and ideas

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Spam or irrelevant content

---

## 🎨 Project Context

This repository contains Blender (.blend) files.

Please keep discussions and contributions focused on:
- Visual quality
- Techniques
- Improvements

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation if needed.

---

## 🤝 Final Note

Keep it simple, respectful, and productive.
